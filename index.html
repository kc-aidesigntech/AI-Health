Why on-island care matters

Neighbors are asking for accessible wellness

Anderson Island’s 1,500 year-round residents rely on ferry travel for the simplest lab tests or prescriptions. A local clinic keeps care timely, affordable, and human.

61.3
Median Age

We support a growing population of seniors who prefer to age in their island homes.

Want On-Island Care

Surveyed households overwhelmingly endorse local services as key to the island’s future.

Building toward a mobile health hub

A phased plan is already underway—co-designed with residents—to bring primary care, diagnostics, and visiting specialists directly to Anderson Island.

  • Community-backed survey data informs each decision.
  • Capital campaign targets a $350K mobile clinic conversion.
  • Integrated partnerships reduce 911 transports and ferry costs.
