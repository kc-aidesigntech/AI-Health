Why your support matters

Donations bring the clinic to life

Your generosity fuels capital investments, staffing, and wellness services designed from community research.

  • Launch the $350K mobile clinic—including exam rooms, diagnostic equipment, and telehealth technology.
  • Expand wellness visits and aging-in-place support for residents facing mobility challenges.
  • Seed the neighbor-care fund so no one delays treatment because of cost.

Every conversation helps refine our services. Volunteer your skills, host a listening session, or share how on-island care would impact your household.

  • Support data entry, grant writing, or campaign communications.
