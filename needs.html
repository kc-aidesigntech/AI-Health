Community Needs Assessment

Our plan is built on data and feedback from our community.

The 2023 Community Survey

In the summer of 2023, AIH conducted a survey to gather information on healthcare needs and barriers. The response was superb, with 262 households responding, representing 570 individuals (nearly 40% of year-round residents).

88% of households agreed that having healthcare services on the island is important to the future of Anderson Island.
