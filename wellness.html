Key services residents rely on

Primary & Preventive Care

Routine visits, chronic care management, immunizations, and wellness coaching delivered locally.

Diagnostics & Lab Work

On-island bloodwork, screenings, and basic imaging to reduce ferry trips for essential testing.

Walk-In & Urgent Support

Minor urgent care, first aid, and telehealth support for timely answers when issues arise.

Mental & Whole-Person Wellness

Behavioral health navigation, caregiver check-ins, and community wellness visits with EMT partners.

Pharmacy Coordination

Medication pick-up support, refills, and education so residents stay on track without mainland travel.

Specialty Pop-Ups

Rotating dental, cardiology, and allied services hosted through regional partnerships.
