About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the community we serve.

Our Mission

"To provide compassionate, accessible, whole-person health and wellness services on Anderson Island. We are dedicated to improving quality of life by delivering exceptional primary care, advancing optimal health outcomes, and fostering community engagement."

Our Vision

"Promoting a healthy and thriving community by empowering individuals to be their best selves."

The Need: An Island in Profile

Our Guiding Principles

Community-Led

Every step centers on the voices of islanders.

Sustainable

Smart growth guided by long-term funding sources (Medicare, levy, insurance).

Partner-Focused

Collaboration with fire, county, and regional provider organizations.

Accessible

Care for all, regardless of income, age, or ability to pay.
