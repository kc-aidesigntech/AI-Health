A critical step in establishing the first-ever clinic on Anderson Island is to braid funding from private and public sources. We are just starting that process now.

Our most immediate goal is to launch a capital campaign to fundraise $350,000. These funds will be used to acquire, convert, and equip a mobile medical clinic vehicle to bring primary care directly to our island.

Your contribution will help us purchase this vital asset and launch Phase 3 of our plan.
